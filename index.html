About me

My name is László Tárkányi, I am from Budapest, Hungary. I'm an international West Coast Swing social dancer, instructor, coach, competition judge, event director and most of all, an enthusiast of the dance.

After beginning in ballroom dancing in 2003, having competition success for years in the boogie-woogie scene, I met West Coast Swing in 2008. In West Coast Swing Ifound what I has been unconsciously seeking, a dance that can be done for almost all genres of music. For me, this dance is the modelling of real life, where you have to obey some easy basic rules, and you can do, create anything, beyond imagination. Nothing is impossible!

I was honored to be a part of the international award-winning Swingshot West Coast Swing Formation team from the beginning. After a successful period in the team, I made my debut to the individual competition scene as well, making it all the way to the All-Star division where I've been competing successfully ever since. In 2016 I graduated as a certified dance instructor, and I'm still further educating myself from world class instructors like Robert Royston, Brandi Guild, Chuck Brown, Jordan Frisbee, Kyle Redd and Browly Adjavon. To further add to my knowledge, I've successfully completed judging education by Chuck Brown, Mario Robau and Kyle Redd, and I've been an active judge in World Swing Dance Council sanctioned competitions since 2016.

When the opportunity presented itself I jumped on the event organizing boat as well. My event, the Westie Spring Thing - Feels like home (formerly Swinghearts Swing Weekend) has been the home-like event for Central European Westies and beyond since 2016. In 2019 the event was also approved by the World Swing Dance Council.

I'm is an enthusiast of teaching the dance to others as well. I have been teaching weekly regular classes, weekend workshops, private classes and coaching sessions since 2013. As time passed I was invited to teach abroad, visit new countries, meeting motivated and motivating students in Austria, Belarus, Czech Republic, Germany, Italy, Israel, the Netherlands, Poland, Slovakia, Sweden, Switzerland, the United States and Ukraine. I am based in Cologne, Germany where I help growing the local community, providing diverse learning and socializing opportunities.
